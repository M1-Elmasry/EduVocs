ABOUT

EduVocs is a revolutionary cross-platform application designed to transform language learning by redefining the way users master vocabulary. The app, currently in its MVP stage, offers real-time learning experiences and eliminates traditional methods like transcribing words onto paper. It features a floating widget for easy accessibility and two sections for knowing and learning vocabularies. The future plans include additional features such as story and article generation based on collected words, along with the ability for users to take exams on their vocabulary. The application is portrayed as a helping tool, a memorization ship for new words, and a valuable resource in the language-learning journey.
